History of the festival

First festival

The very first amateur sculpture festival was staged in 1999, and was co-ordinated by Brendan Doyle of the Cockleshell Art Gallery. Brendan had first seen sandsculpture the previous year on holiday in italy, and noticed that the sand used was very similar to the sand on Duncannon Beach.

First Artists

The gallery commissioned artists Daniel Doyle and Fergus Mulvaney to come and test the sand for suitability and to act as resident artists for the duration of the festival. Both artists were studying sculpture at D.I.T. at this time, and also members of the Irish Sand & Snow Sculpture collective.
