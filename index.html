How to make a sandsculpture


Step 1

Gather empty buckets for water and sandpile-building, spray bottle for water, carving tools (be creative - trowels, spades, knives, forks, spoons, pallette knives, twisted wire), shells/driftwood for decoration.


Step 2

Create a mound of damp sand in roughly the height and width of your proposed sculpture. Constantly pack the sand down to make it denser with each bucket of sand you add to build the pile.

Step 3

Gently remove sand from the pile to get the general shape right. Start with the highest point first and work down, so falling sand only lands on uncarved surfaces. Use bigger tools to rough out the shape, smaller tools for details.


Step 4

Carve out the focal points first — the bits that tell the story — then go back and add detail to the rest. Once a section is finished, keep misting with water to prevent sand collapsing. Finish with decoration.
